Artificial Intelligence in Medicine

Early Cancer Detection Using Machine Learning

What is Artificial Intelligence?

Artificial Intelligence (AI) allows computers to learn patterns from data. In healthcare, AI analyzes medical scans and patient data faster than humans.

How AI Detects Cancer

  • Examines X-rays and MRI scans
  • Identifies abnormal cell growth
  • Predicts cancer risk
  • Assists doctors in diagnosis

Why This Matters

Early detection greatly increases survival rates. AI can find cancer years earlier than traditional screening methods.
